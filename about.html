About this website


I'm Lucien, and I'm the co-founder of a trading app called OhMyGeorge. For benchmarking purposes I spend quite a lot of time trying all the stock, forex, and binary trading apps available in the market - and this is how, as a fun project, I eventually put up this review website.

This website is supported by referrals rather than ugly ads, so I may earn a commission if you signup through some of the links.

I'm especially fond of great UI/UX, but most trading apps aren't exactly impressive regarding that aspect, so that's what initially motivated me to build Oh My George. It's only a demo trading app at the moment (real-time market data but virtual money). We are hoping to make it transactional soon (i.e. allowing you to connect a real-money brokerage account of your choice).

I also freelance on UI/UX missions, so if you're looking for a unique blend of app design skills and financial market acumen, check out my services.

If you have any questions or comments, just send me an email to hello@besttradingapp.com or use my contact page. I love exchanging ideas about how to improve Best Trading App or, of course, OhMyGeorge!
