Account Validation
The questionnaire went a bit deeper than the usual check, with some Q&A along the way to validate that I understood the concept of leverage. I wonder what would have happened if I had answered wrong! That was quite weird... Anyway, I could scan my ID and bank statement (as proof of adress) with my phone. The ID was validated very quickly, while the bank statement got rejected because I took a photo of my computer screen. Once I sent them the PDF version of the bank statement, it got validated right away.
Funding Process
Interestingly, Plus500 accepts PayPal deposits, which is not so common (usually the e-wallet proposed is either Srill or Neteller). I chose, however, to deposit via credit card, which worked as a charm. The funds were immedialty available for trading. Withdrawals
My withdrawal attempt was submitted instantly, and it got processed as soon as the next day. Interestingly, plus500 accepted to transfer on my card more than the initial amount I deposited with it (just a few cents, but still), which is usually never the case with other brokers because of the transfer cost (if the amount is lower than the initial amount deposited, then the operationis a "refund", and it does not cost anything to the broker). Cleary here, plus500 deserves the verified badge.
Trading Experience
Not terrible (it wasn't buggy), but very poor experience, to be honest. There's no possibility to add indicators! The charts look very vintage and basic explanations are missing (like the minimum size for a trade, you have to figure that out yourself by trying different amounts). Overall, this app was designed based on the assumptions that users would plan their trades from the web station, and only check on them from their mobile, but not make trading decisions on the go. One good thing to note is the possibility to have guaranteed stop loss, which is not something all CFD brokers offer yet (but it's not free, look at the "adjustements" I was charged for my trade).
Customer Support
Great experience: no phone call (I like that), useful automated emailing for important matters (deposit status, account verification process explanation, etc.) and super fast replies with the in-app customer support live chat. That's what we want from a broker, nothing more, nothing less. On this aspect plus500 will be appreciated by the digital natives.
