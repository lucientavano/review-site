IQ OPTION REVIEW

Here is a full-feature review of the IQ Option trading app. It's the official video for now - I have recorded my own and will publish it soon.

iqoption.com
Account Validation
I could scan my ID and bank statement (as proof of adress) with my phone.
Funding Process
Just $10 required deposit to start, and it got instantly credited to my account.
Trading Experience
The app is packed with features, but nonetheless very easy/intuitive to navigate.
Customer Support
A few minutes after signing up, someone called me (which I usually don't like). But to my surprise it was not a sales speech or a sweet talk to make me deposit (more) money. The focus was exclusively on explaining me the app's technical features despite no attempt from my side to discourage any sales speech. Nice! But I don't think calling prospective users is necessary. Let us live our lives!
Withdrawals
Both the withdrawal attemps I made were approved and processed instantly (despite the fact that my account wasn't fully verified yet because I still had to submit a proof of address!). I had, however, to use an e-wallet (I use Skrill, since PayPal is rarely accepted by brokers) to get my profits (what was in excess of the initial deposit that I had done with my regular credit card). Indeed, the maximum amount I could withdraw back to my credit card was limited to the initial amount I deposited. This is for IQ Option a way to avoid the high bank card's fee. I'm fine with that, I think it's fair since IQ Option didn't pass on to me any of the deposit or withdrawal fees that must have been charged by the payment gateway.
